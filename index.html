Noli Me Tangere Characters
Chrisostomo Ibarra Spanish-Filipino descent, Wealthy, studied in Europe for 7 years
Father Damaso Spanish Friar, Arrogant and pedantic
Maria Clara Well-regarded in San Diego, is enganged with Chrisostomo Ibarra
Captain Tiago (Don Santiago) Extremely Rich and Wealthy, Well-respected and part of the country's elite, Close with the country's clergy members
Rafael Ibarra Chrisostomo's late father, Died in prison, Falsely-accused of commiting a homicide
